Ladakh Group Trip (7N/8D)

Experience the majestic landscapes of Ladakh with Backpack and Beyond

Backpack and Beyond invites you on an unforgettable 7N/8D journey through Ladakh — from the high-altitude deserts of Nubra and magical Pangong Lake to the lesser-known village of Hanle. Discover monasteries, culture, Himalayan passes, and starry skies on this perfectly curated adventure for group travellers.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Arrival in Leh

Upon arrival at Leh Airport (Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport), our driver will transfer you to your pre-booked hotel.

Check-in and relax to acclimatize to the cold weather and high altitude of Leh. This rest day is essential to prepare you for the upcoming adventure.

Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 2: Exploring Leh & Sham Valley

The driver will pick you up from the hotel for a full-day excursion to Sham Valley.

Magnetic Hill & Hall of Fame

Experience the gravity-defying Magnetic Hill and visit the war memorial museum built by the Indian Army.

Zanskar-Indus Confluence & SECMOL

Witness the confluence of Zanskar and Indus Rivers and visit the SECMOL School (optional).

Return to Leh in the evening. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung-La

After breakfast, check out and depart for Nubra Valley via Khardung-La, the world's highest motorable road.

Khardung-La Pass

Stop at the iconic pass at 18,380 ft and enjoy the snow-capped landscapes.

Nubra Valley & Camel Safari

Explore the sand dunes and take a ride on double-humped Bactrian camels. Optional visit to Diskit Monastery.

Overnight stay in camps at Nubra Valley.

Day 4: Nubra Valley - Turtuk Excursion (Optional)

Choose between two options for today's exploration:

  • Option A: Drive to Turtuk - a scenic and remote Balti village near the Indo-Pak border.
  • Option B: Stay back in Nubra to explore Sumur Village, Panamik Hot Springs, and Yarab Tso Lake.

Overnight stay in Hunder or Diskit, Nubra Valley.

Day 5: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Check out after breakfast and depart for Pangong Lake via Shyok route.

Pangong Lake

Marvel at the magical lake that changes colours from blue to green to red. A must-visit spot from iconic Bollywood scenes.

Overnight stay at campsite near Pangong Lake.

Day 6: Pangong to Hanle (Special Permit Required)

Early morning departure for Hanle via Chushul, Tsaga La, and Loma.

Hanle Monastery

Visit the remote and peaceful Hanle Monastery. Enjoy the high-altitude desert landscape.

Overnight stay at guesthouse or campsite in Hanle.

Day 7: Hanle to Leh

After breakfast, drive back to Leh via Nyoma, Mahe, and Chumathang. Enjoy scenic drives and natural hot springs en route.

Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 8: Departure from Leh

Pack your bags and check out from your hotel. The driver will transfer you to Leh Airport for your return journey.

Tour ends with unforgettable memories of Ladakh.
