Kedarnath Group Trip (3N/4D)

Spiritual Journey to the Heart of the Himalayas with Backpack and Beyond

Backpack and Beyond is your gateway to authentic and immersive travel experiences. We specialize in group tours that combine comfort, adventure, and surprise elements to make each trip unforgettable.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Haridwar / Dehradun ‚Üí Guptkashi / Sonprayag

Pickup from Haridwar or Dehradun (morning). Drive to Guptkashi or Sonprayag (approx. 8-10 hours / 210-230 km).

En route visit: Rudraprayag, Devprayag confluence.

Rudraprayag

Spiritual town at the confluence of Mandakini and Alaknanda rivers, rich in local tradition.

Devprayag

The sacred confluence where Alaknanda and Bhagirathi meet to form the Ganga.

Check-in at hotel in Guptkashi/Sonprayag. Overnight stay and dinner.

Day 2: Guptkashi / Sonprayag ‚Üí Kedarnath (Trek)

Early morning drive to Gaurikund (start of trek).

Trek to Kedarnath (18 km; options include foot, pony, palki, or helicopter from Phata/Sersi).

Gaurikund

Base point of Kedarnath trek, known for its hot springs and natural beauty.

Kedarnath Trek

Beautiful Himalayan trek through pine forests and mountain views.

Reach Kedarnath by evening. Visit Shri Kedarnath Temple for evening aarti/darshan.

Overnight stay in Kedarnath (dormitory/camps/lodge).

Day 3: Kedarnath ‚Üí Gaurikund ‚Üí Guptkashi / Sonprayag

Early morning darshan at the temple (optional).

Kedarnath Temple

The sacred shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, a powerful pilgrimage site.

Trek down to Gaurikund and drive back to Guptkashi/Sonprayag.

Check-in and relax at hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 4: Guptkashi / Sonprayag ‚Üí Haridwar / Dehradun

After breakfast, drive back to Haridwar/Dehradun.

Return Journey

Enjoy scenic mountain views as you travel back through Uttarakhand.

Drop at railway station or airport.
