Rishikesh Group Trip (2N/3D)

Spiritual calm, river thrill, and café charm with Backpack and Beyond

Welcome to Rishikesh — the yoga capital and the gateway to the Himalayas. Backpack and Beyond invites you on a trip that balances soulful experiences with adrenaline-fueled adventure. From Ganga Aarti to river rafting and a surprise off-beat destination, this 3-day escape offers the perfect blend of calm and thrill.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Delhi → Rishikesh (Overnight Journey)

Depart from Delhi around 10:00 PM by Tempo Traveller/Cab/Train. Overnight journey (~6-7 hours, ~240 km).

Morning arrival in Rishikesh. Check-in at hotel/camp between 8:00-10:00 AM. Freshen up and rest.

Laxman Jhula & Ram Jhula

Iconic suspension bridges with spiritual charm and panoramic river views.

Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat

Attend the mesmerizing evening Aarti (6-7 PM) with chants and diyas on the Ganga.

Evening: Explore local markets for souvenirs, spiritual items, and books. Overnight stay at hotel/camp.

Day 2: Adventure & Ashrams

After early breakfast, enjoy an adrenaline-packed river rafting session (9-11 AM) - choose from 9km / 16km / 26km routes.

Beatles Ashram

Explore this graffiti-filled spiritual site made famous by The Beatles (entry fee applicable).

Parmarth Niketan Ashram

A serene spiritual haven by the river offering peaceful ambiance and yoga retreats.

Overnight stay at hotel/camp in Rishikesh.

Day 3: Rishikesh → Delhi

Start the day with a peaceful walk along the Ganga river.

Optional: Visit Neelkanth Mahadev Temple (~1.5 hrs one way) if time permits.

Check out by 11:00 AM and begin the return journey to Delhi (approx. 6-7 hours).
