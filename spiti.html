Spiti Valley Group Expedition (7N/8D)

High-altitude adventure, ancient monasteries, and surreal Himalayan landscapes

Welcome to Spiti ‚Äî the land between Tibet and India, where rugged beauty meets timeless spirituality. Backpack and Beyond invites you on an unforgettable expedition through remote mountain villages, ancient monasteries, and pristine lakes. From the mystical mummies of Gue to the sparkling waters of Chandratal and postcard-perfect villages like Langza and Komic, this 8-day journey is crafted for explorers who seek both peace and adventure.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Delhi ‚Üí Shimla (Overnight Journey)

Board your AC Volvo from Delhi at 08:00 PM for an overnight journey to Shimla.

Day 2: Shimla - The Apple Capital of India

Morning pickup from Shimla Volvo stand, check-in to the hotel. Rest for a while and then explore:

Himalayan Mini Zoo

Home to Felines, Antelopes, Monal, and other exotic birds.

Green & Fagu Valley

Enjoy panoramic Himalayan views from these scenic locations.

Evening stroll at Mall Road, Ridge, Christ Church, and Lakkar Bazar. Overnight stay at Shimla/Narkanda.

Day 3: Shimla/Narkanda ‚Üí Sangla/Chhitkul

Post breakfast, drive towards the stunning Kinnaur Valley via the Sutlej river route. Highlights:

Kamru Fort

Ancient fort with shrines to 33 deities and Kamakhya Devi brought from Assam.

Chhitkul - Last Village at Indo-Tibetan Border

Explore the pristine village along the Baspa river. Overnight stay at Kalpa.

Day 4: Sangla ‚Üí Tabo

Drive to Spiti Valley via Nako and Gue Village. Highlights include:

Nako Lake & Monastery

Scenic lake and monastery with Buddhist teachings and murals.

Gue Mummy & Tabo Monastery

Visit the 500-year-old mummy at Gue and the ancient Tabo Monastery ‚Äî 'Ajanta of the Himalayas'.

Overnight stay near Tabo.

Day 5: Tabo ‚Üí Kaza (via Dhankar & Key Monastery)

After breakfast, drive to Kaza with stops at:

Dhankar Monastery

1000-year-old monastery perched over a cliff at the confluence of Spiti and Pin rivers.

Key Monastery & Chicham Bridge

Visit the iconic monastery and Asia's highest bridge overlooking a deep gorge.

Overnight stay at Kaza.

Day 6: Explore Kaza (Langza, Hikkim, Komic)

Full day sightseeing around Kaza's high-altitude villages:

Langza, Komic & Hikkim

Visit the world's highest villages, fossil grounds, and the world's highest post office at Hikkim.

Explore Kaza local market in the evening. Overnight stay at Kaza homestay.

Day 7: Kaza ‚Üí Chandratal

Travel to the stunning crescent-shaped Chandratal Lake via Kunzum La. Short trek of 2km to reach the lake. Enjoy stargazing at night.

Chandratal Lake

Also known as Moon Lake, known for changing hues throughout the day. Overnight camping nearby.

Day 8: Chandratal ‚Üí Manali ‚Üí Delhi (Overnight Volvo)

Depart Chandratal for Manali via Atal Tunnel and Solang Valley. Check-in and explore Manali:

Manali Local

Explore Hadimba Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, and Mall Road. Volvo departs at 06:00 PM for Delhi.

Tour ends with unforgettable memories and stories to tell!
