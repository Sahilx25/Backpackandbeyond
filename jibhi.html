Jibhi & Tirthan (3N/4D)

Explore Himachal's Hidden Gem with Backpack and Beyond
Starting from ₹8,200 per person

Backpack and Beyond is your gateway to authentic and immersive travel experiences. We specialize in group tours that combine comfort, adventure, and surprise elements to make each trip unforgettable.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Delhi to Jibhi (Volvo/Cab)

Arrival at Jibhi via cab or Volvo. Meet our representative & check in to the hotel or homestay. Unwind amidst the serene mountains & prepare for a peaceful & scenic Himalayan retreat.

Arrival at Aut/Banjar & Transfer to Jibhi

Reach Aut or Banjar via Volvo/Cab, continue journey to Jibhi. Check in and relax at your stay.

Jibhi Waterfall & Café Hopping

Take a refreshing walk to the beautiful Jibhi Waterfall and explore cozy local cafés nearby.

Bonfire & Dinner

Enjoy a warm bonfire session followed by a delicious dinner under the stars.

Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Jalori Pass & Serolsar Lake Trek
Jalori Pass

Start your day with breakfast, then drive to the scenic Jalori Pass (~10,800 ft) offering stunning Himalayan views.

Serolsar Lake Trek

Begin a 5 km trek (~2 hrs one way) through dense forest to the serene Serolsar Lake. Enjoy a packed lunch at the lake.

Return to Jibhi

After a peaceful lakeside experience, trek back and return to Jibhi for dinner and overnight stay.

Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Chehni Kothi & Shringa Rishi Temple
Shringa Rishi Temple

Visit the beautiful and sacred temple of Shringa Rishi, the deity of Banjar Valley.

Chehni Kothi Trek

Trek to the ancient 1,500-year-old tower temple made of wood and stone – a remarkable architectural wonder.

Gushaini Village Exploration

Explore the serene Gushaini village. Optional activities include angling, bird watching, or simply soaking in the beauty.

Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Tirthan Valley Exploration
Chhoie Waterfall Hike

Embark on a scenic hike to the hidden Chhoie Waterfall, nestled amidst lush greenery.

GHNP Gate Visit (Optional)

Optional visit to the Great Himalayan National Park gate (permit required). A chance to spot rare flora and fauna.

Evening by Tirthan River

Relax by the riverside, enjoy the soothing sound of the flowing water and end your day with a hearty dinner.

Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Transfer & Goodbye Transfer to Aut/Chandigarh/Delhi. Tour ends with unforgettable memories of the mountains.
