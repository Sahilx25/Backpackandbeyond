Himachal Explorer (8N/9D)

Snow-capped peaks, pine valleys, ancient temples, and adventure thrills

Discover the magical charm of Himachal Pradesh ‚Äî a land where snow-kissed mountains meet colonial towns, vibrant culture, and pristine valleys. Backpack and Beyond presents an immersive 9-day journey through Shimla, Kufri, Manali, Solang, Kasol, Manikaran, Dharamshala, McLeod Ganj, Dalhousie, and Khajjiar. From bustling markets and adventure spots to serene monasteries and forested trails, this trip is curated for explorers, families, couples, and solo travelers eager to soak in the true spirit of the Himalayas.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Delhi to Shimla

Morning Arrival: Arrive at Delhi railway station or airport. Meet our executive and transfer to Shimla.

Check-in & Relax

Transfer to your hotel, check-in and unwind. Enjoy a delicious dinner.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Shimla.
Dinner Only

Day 2: Kufri & Shimla Local Sightseeing

Morning: After breakfast, explore Shimla - Mall Road, Wooden Market, Christ Church, The Ridge, and more.

Kufri Excursion

Visit Green Valley, Fun World, Chini Bungalow, Fagu Valley. Horse or Yak rides optional.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Shimla.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 3: Shimla to Manali via Kullu

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Manali. En route visit Sundernagar Lake, Pandoh Dam, Vaishno Devi Temple at Kullu.

Kullu Visit

Option to enjoy river rafting. Continue drive to Manali.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Manali.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 4: Manali Local & Solang Valley

Morning: Visit Hadimba Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Tibetan Monastery, and Club House.

Solang Valley

Enjoy adventure activities and visit Nehru Kund and Nag Temple.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Manali.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 5: Manali - Manikaran - Kasol

Morning: Visit Manikaran Gurudwara and hot springs. Later, proceed to Kasol for sightseeing.

Manikaran & Kasol

Explore Kasol's charm before returning to Manali.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Manali.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 6: Manali to Dharamshala

Morning: Check out and drive to Dharamshala. En route visit Palampur Tea Gardens, Baijnath and Chamunda Devi Temples.

Palampur & Temples

Scenic route with spiritual and natural stops.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Dharamshala.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 7: Dharamshala to Dalhousie

Morning: Sightseeing in Dharamshala including McLeod Ganj, Dalai Lama Temple, and more. Later drive to Dalhousie.

McLeod Ganj

Visit Dal Lake, Bhagsu Nag, Tibetan Monastery, and Cricket Stadium.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Dalhousie.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 8: Dalhousie - Khajjiar

Morning: Visit Kalatop and Khajjiar. Option for paragliding and camping. Explore Dalhousie's churches and viewpoints.

Khajjiar & Local Sites

Visit Panchpula, Subhash Baoli, Gandhi Chowk, and Wildlife Sanctuary.

Overnight Stay: At hotel in Dalhousie.
Breakfast & Dinner

Day 9: Dalhousie to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, check out from hotel and transfer back to Delhi. Drop at airport or railway station.

End of Tour.
Breakfast Only
