Kerala Explorer Journey (7N/8D)

Backwaters, hill stations, beaches, and vibrant local culture

Welcome to Kerala ‚Äî God's Own Country ‚Äî where serene backwaters, lush tea plantations, golden beaches, and rich cultural heritage come together to offer an unforgettable travel experience. Backpack and Beyond invites you on a carefully curated 8-day journey through Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey, and Kovalam. From houseboat stays and spice plantations to traditional Kathakali performances and coastal sunsets, this trip is crafted for travelers seeking a blend of relaxation, adventure, and authentic encounters.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Kochi Arrival & Sightseeing

Airport pickup and hotel check-in. Explore the colonial charm of Kochi:

Mattancherry Palace

Portuguese-built palace known for Kerala murals and royal artifacts.

Jewish Synagogue

One of the oldest synagogues in India, built in 1568 with hand-painted tiles.

Enjoy local shopping. Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 2: Arrival at Munnar

Drive through scenic tea plantations to Munnar. Check-in and leisure day with your partner.

Road to Munnar

Enjoy lush greenery and winding mountain roads en route to Munnar.

Day 3: Munnar Sightseeing

Full-day sightseeing in Munnar's natural beauty and attractions:

Eravikulam National Park

Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and stunning viewpoints.

Tata Tea Museum

Learn about tea processing and enjoy tasting authentic Munnar tea.

Visit Photo Point, Rose Garden, Echo Point, Mattupetty Dam & more. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 4: Munnar ‚Üí Thekkady

Scenic drive through cardamom hills to Thekkady. Post check-in:

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Experience the jungle with an exciting Jeep Safari.

Bamboo Rafting (Optional)

Adventure-packed rafting experience inside the forest (at extra cost).

Overnight stay at Thekkady.

Day 5: Thekkady ‚Üí Alleppey (Backwaters)

Post breakfast, drive to Alleppey. Check-in to traditional houseboat:

Houseboat Experience

Enjoy a scenic backwater cruise with lunch & dinner on board.

Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 6: Alleppey ‚Üí Kovalam

After breakfast, check out from the houseboat and head to the beach town of Kovalam:

Kovalam Beach

Relax and rejuvenate by the sea. Explore cafes and beaches.

Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 7: Kovalam Sightseeing

Beach hopping day with stunning sunsets:

Lighthouse Beach

Climb to the top for panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

Hawah & Samudra Beaches

Peaceful coastal spots ideal for a romantic evening walk.

Enjoy the sunset with your partner. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 8: Departure Day

Post breakfast, drive to Thiruvananthapuram Airport for your onward journey with memories of God's Own Country.
