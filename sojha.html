Sojha Group Trip (3N/4D)

Explore the hidden Himalayan gem with Backpack and Beyond

Backpack and Beyond brings you an offbeat Himalayan experience in the serene village of Sojha. Ideal for nature lovers, this 3N/4D itinerary includes treks, waterfalls, ancient forts, and local culture — perfect for group getaways.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Arrival at AUT & Sojha Stay

Arrive at AUT (from Delhi/Chandigarh). Check into your pre-booked homestay/hotel.

Relax, enjoy lunch with a view (on your own).

Raghupur Fort Ruins

Explore the ruins of Raghupur Fort offering 360-degree views of valleys and Himalayan ranges.

Sojha Village Walk

Take a short walk around the village to soak in its rustic charm and traditional wooden architecture.

Sunset: Watch the sun go down from the Sojha viewpoint.
Overnight stay at homestay/hotel in Sojha.

Day 2: Jalori Pass + Serolsar Lake Trek

Early morning departure for Jalori Pass. Begin the scenic trek to Serolsar Lake.

Serolsar Lake

Peaceful lake ideal for picnic and relaxation, surrounded by thick forests and mountain views.

Budhi Nagin Temple

Visit the ancient temple near the lake and enjoy the stunning snowcapped scenery.

Trek back to Jalori and return to Sojha by evening. Overnight stay in Sojha.

Day 3: Waterfall Hike + Optional Tirthan Valley Visit

Start your morning with a short hike to the beautiful Sojha waterfall.

Waterfall Hike

Enjoy the calming sound of water and the lush forest surrounding the hidden waterfall.

Optional: Explore Tirthan Valley if time and weather permit.
Enjoy dinner at your pre-booked stay.

Day 4: Departure

Wake up early for one last Himalayan sunrise and have a hearty breakfast.

Check out from your stay. Our cab will drop you at AUT Station for your return journey to Delhi or next destination.

Tour ends with warm memories of Sojha!
