Chopta - Tungnath Adventure (2N/3D)

Meadows, spiritual peaks, alpine lakes, and Himalayan serenity

Backpack and Beyond invites you on a thoughtfully designed 3-day journey through Chopta, Tungnath, Chandrashila, and Deoria Tal. From scenic treks and spiritual heights to reflective lakes and starlit bonfires, this trip is ideal for nature lovers, adventurers, and those seeking peace in the lap of the Himalayas.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Arrival in Chopta

Morning Arrival: Reach Chopta by noon. Check-in: Settle into your campsite or homestay.

Chopta Bugyal

Explore the alpine meadows or enjoy a peaceful forest walk.

Bonfire

Evening tea and snacks around a warm bonfire with serene views.

Overnight Stay: At the campsite/homestay.

Day 2: Trek to Tungnath & Chandrashila

Early Morning: Begin the 3.5 km trek to Tungnath Temple, the highest Shiva temple in the world.

Tungnath Temple

A sacred high-altitude temple offering a spiritual and scenic experience.

Chandrashila Peak

Additional 1 km trek to the summit at 3,690 meters for panoramic Himalayan views.

Return to Chopta and relax. Overnight Stay: At the campsite/homestay.

Day 3: Deoria Tal Trek & Return to Delhi

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Sari Village - the starting point of the Deoria Tal trek.

Deoria Tal

Reflective lake surrounded by forest, known for its scenic beauty and mountain views.

Trek back to Sari Village. Lunch and departure to Delhi. Arrival in Delhi by 6:00 AM next morning.
