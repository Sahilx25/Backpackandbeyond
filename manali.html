Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Manali Arrival + Local Sightseeing
Hadimba Devi Temple

An ancient cave temple dedicated to Hidimbi Devi, surrounded by cedar forest. A peaceful spiritual stop in Manali.

Vashisht Hot Springs

Naturally occurring hot springs believed to have healing powers. Enjoy public and private bath options.

Tibetan Monastery

A serene Buddhist site with vibrant Tibetan architecture, great for meditation and shopping souvenirs.

Mall Road

Manali's bustling street for shopping, cafés, and evening walks. Try local food and soak in the vibe.

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventures
Paragliding

Fly over valleys and mountains with professional guides. One of the top adventure activities in Manali.

Nehru Kund

A natural spring named after Jawaharlal Nehru, offering scenic views and refreshing Himalayan water.

Nag Temple

Peaceful and mystical temple in the forest dedicated to the serpent deity. A glimpse into local Himachali traditions.

Day 3: Naggar Castle & Art Gallery
Naggar Castle

Historic castle built in the 15th century with traditional architecture and scenic views of the Kullu Valley.

Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery

Artworks and personal belongings of the Russian artist Roerich, set in a peaceful estate amidst pine forests.
