Arrival at Manali via Volvo or cab. Meet our representative and check in to the hotel. Relax and get ready for an adventurous journey in the Himalayas.

An ancient cave temple dedicated to Hidimbi Devi, surrounded by cedar forest. A peaceful spiritual stop in Manali.

Vashisht Hot Springs

Naturally occurring hot springs believed to have healing powers. Enjoy public and private bath options.

Tibetan Monastery

A serene Buddhist site with vibrant Tibetan architecture, great for meditation and shopping souvenirs.
