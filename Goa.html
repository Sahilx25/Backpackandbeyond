Goa Group Trip (3N/4D)

Relax by the stunning beaches with Backpack and Beyond

Backpack and Beyond is your gateway to authentic and immersive travel experiences. We specialize in couple and group tours that combine comfort, exploration, and surprise elements to make each trip unforgettable.

Itinerary Section (Timeline Format)

Day 1: Goa Arrival

Arrival at Goa Airport. Meet our representative who will assist you with hotel check-in. Rest and prepare for an exciting journey ahead.

Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing
Candolim Beach

Visit the famous Candolim Beach, a great spot for relaxation and water activities.

Calangute Beach

Explore Calangute Beach, known for its lively atmosphere and beautiful sunsets.

Baga Beach

Enjoy Baga Beach, famous for its nightlife, shacks, and water sports.

Anjuna Beach

Visit the vibrant Anjuna Beach, popular for its flea market and party scene.

Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: South Goa Sightseeing
Colva Beach

Relax at the serene Colva Beach, ideal for long walks and scenic views.

Miramar Beach

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to the airport or station for your onward journey, taking back beautiful memories.
